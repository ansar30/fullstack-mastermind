# 90-Day Full-Stack Mastery Roadmap

A comprehensive plan to become a proficient full-stack developer with deep knowledge of the MERN stack, DevOps, and modern best practices.

## Overview

- **Duration:** 90 Days (13 weeks)
- **Daily Commitment:** 3-4 hours
- **Target:** Full-Stack Developer Mastery
- **Focus:** Deep understanding, production-ready skills, portfolio projects
