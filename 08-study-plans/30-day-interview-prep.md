# 30-Day Interview Preparation Plan

A focused, intensive plan to prepare for full-stack developer interviews in 30 days.

## Overview

- **Duration:** 30 Days
- **Daily Commitment:** 4-6 hours
- **Target:** Full-Stack Developer (MERN) Position
- **Focus:** Interview readiness, practical skills, problem-solving

- [ ] Review ES6+ features (arrow functions, destructuring, spread/rest)
- [ ] Closures, hoisting, and scope
- [ ] `this` keyword and binding

**Afternoon (2 hours):**
- [ ] Solve 3 easy JavaScript problems on LeetCode
- [ ] Practice: Array methods (map, filter, reduce)

**Evening (1 hour):**
- [ ] Review common interview questions

## Tips for Success

### Do's ✅
- **Consistency is key** - Study daily
- **Practice coding** - Don't just read
- **Explain out loud** - Helps solidify understanding
- **Build projects** - Practical experience matters
- **Take breaks** - Avoid burnout
- **Sleep well** - Your brain needs rest
- **Stay healthy** - Exercise, eat well

### Don'ts ❌
- **Don't cram** - Spaced repetition is better
- **Don't skip basics** - Strong foundation is crucial
- **Don't just watch tutorials** - Code along
- **Don't neglect soft skills** - Communication matters
- **Don't compare** - Focus on your own progress
- **Don't give up** - Persistence pays off

## Adjust as Needed

This plan is intensive. If you need more time:
- Extend to 45 or 60 days
- Focus on your weak areas
- Reduce daily hours if needed
- Quality > Quantity

**Remember:** Everyone learns at their own pace. The goal is to be well-prepared, not to rush through everything.
